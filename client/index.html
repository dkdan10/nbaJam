Navigate menus with arrow keys. Select with Enter.

Instructions

Online:

Right Arrow: Move Right

Left Arrow: Move Left

Up Arrow: Jump

Space Bar: Shoot. Hold to shoot.

Offline:

Right Arrow: Move Player 2 Right

Left Arrow: Move Player 2 Left

Up Arrow: Player 2 Jump

/: Player 2 shoot. Hold to shoot.

d: Move Player 1 Right

a: Move Player 1 Left

w: Player 1 Jump

q: Player 1 shoot. Hold to shoot.
