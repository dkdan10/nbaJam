Instructions

Online:

Right Arrow: Move Right

Left Arrow: Move left

Up Arrow: Jump

Space Bar: Shoot ball. Hold to shoot

Offline:

Right Arrow: Move Player 2 Right

Left Arrow: Move Player 2 left

Up Arrow: Player 2 Jump

/: Player 2 shoot ball. Hold to shoot

d: Move Player 1 Right

a: Move Player 1 left

w: Arrow: Player 1 Jump

q: Player 1 shoot ball. Hold to shoot
